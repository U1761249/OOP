The generator will run from the treasure room so that all generated dungeons are possible to complete. 
The treasure room will spawn in a random cell and have only one door.

Generation rules:
The first room must be the treasure room
A room must have a door leading to the room it was genrated from
A room should me more than likely to have a second door
A room cannot have a door which leads to the wall of another
A room may be a dead end.
x1 rooms cannot have a west door
9x and 0 rooms cannot have a north door
x0 rooms cannot have an east door
Rooms 1-10 cannot have a south door
The player can only spawn in an empty room
No monsters can spawn in the treasure room
Room ID's may be left unused if a room cannot be generated in that position
